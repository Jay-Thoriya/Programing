Jubilee

The Jubilee has a dreadnought shape and takes its name from the biblical concept of the Year of Jubilee where all debts were to be forgiven every 50 years. The Jubilee has a large, focused sound and excels in the hands of rhythm players.

Pearl

The Pearl is the newest addition to the DA family. Uganda is often called the ‘Pearl of Africa’, and we thought this a fitting title for our parlor guitar. A small body with a big sound, the Pearl will be perfectly suited to folksy, blues and roots players.
